Who we are

Friday Initiatives is a boutique data consultancy that helps companies navigate their unique data journey. We'll empower you to transform your data into a strategic advantage by helping you understand what data you have, unlock and protect its value, and leverage it strategically.

Headquartered in London, we bring together a world-class team of experts covering the US, UK, EU, Singapore, China, Australia and New Zealand.

Lauren/Emma Photo

Headquartered in London, we bring together a world-class team of experts covering the US, UK, EU, Singapore, China, Australia and New Zealand.

Our approach is cross-functional, free of jargon, and rooted in next-generation techniques and technology to deliver the best possible results.

By investing holistically in your data landscape, your business will be on the front foot of regulation, moving from reluctant compliance to embracing it as a competitive advantage. You'll also have the data to attract new clients, grow revenue and embrace the opportunities presented by AI.

Meet our team

Friday Initiatives doesn't just have its own team. We have also built a network of partners, who are the best at what they do when it comes to data. This includes experts in data governance, AI, data science, project management, change management, privacy, marketing, ad-operations and law.

How we work
